Who We Are

Passionate About
Wilpattu's Wild Heart

Willpattu Wild Safari was born from a deep love for nature and the extraordinary biodiversity of Wilpattu National Park — Sri Lanka's largest and oldest national park. Our mission is simple: to deliver the most authentic, ethical, and exhilarating wildlife experiences in the island.

Our team of expert trackers and naturalists have spent years navigating the 1,317 square kilometres of Wilpattu's forests, wetlands, and grasslands. We know the animals, the trees, and the trails intimately — and we're passionate about sharing this knowledge with every visitor who joins us.

We believe that responsible tourism and wildlife conservation go hand in hand. Every safari we conduct contributes to the local community and supports the protection of Wilpattu's incredible ecosystems.
