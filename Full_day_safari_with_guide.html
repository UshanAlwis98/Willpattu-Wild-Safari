Full Day Safari Experience

All Inclusive Full Day Safari- With Guide

This full-day safari in Wilpattu National Park is ideal for those who want to experience wildlife more deeply. With an experienced guide and a comfortable 4x4 jeep, you will explore different areas of the park with opportunities to spot leopards, sloth bears, elephants, and a variety of birds. The package includes breakfast and lunch, and you can also stop near beautiful "Villus" (lakes) for relaxation and photography.

Nestled within the vast, ancient wilderness of Sri Lanka's North Western Province, Wilpattu National Park stands as the island's largest and most historically significant wildlife reserve — a landscape shaped by centuries of untouched nature. Our all-inclusive full day safari invites you to experience every dimension of this extraordinary ecosystem, from the misty silence of the early morning villus to the amber glow of the evening sky as the park settles into dusk.

Departing at 6:15 AM, your journey begins with a comfortable Toyota 4×4 pickup from your hotel, rolling into the park just as the wildlife begins its daily rhythm. Over twelve immersive hours, your experienced driver-guide will navigate the park's most productive routes — pausing at waterhole clearings, scanning the treeline for leopards, and waiting patiently at open grasslands where elephants graze undisturbed. The pace is deliberately unhurried, giving you time to truly absorb the sights, sounds, and stillness of wild Sri Lanka.

Every detail of your day has been carefully arranged so that nothing breaks the magic. A full breakfast is served inside the park at sunrise, followed by a hearty lunch at a scenic rest point midway through your drive. Afternoon snacks, fresh fruits, homemade cake, and Ceylon tea are packed into a cool box alongside chilled water and refreshments. Binoculars, entrance tickets, jeep fees, taxes, and all service charges are included — there is nothing left to organise, and nothing left to pay.

Leopards, Elephants & Sloth Bears Wilpattu holds one of the highest leopard densities in Asia, alongside large herds of elephants and the elusive sloth bear, regularly sighted along forested trails.
Expert Local Driver-Guides Your guide knows every trail, every seasonal watering hole, and every animal behaviour pattern — dramatically increasing your chances of remarkable wildlife encounters.
Fully Catered from Dawn to Dusk Breakfast, lunch, snacks, fruits, cake, tea, and chilled drinks are all included. You arrive ready to explore and leave with nothing but memories to carry home.
Private & Flexible Experience With a maximum of six guests per jeep, your safari is intimate and unhurried. Routes are adjusted in real time based on fresh animal movements reported across the park.
